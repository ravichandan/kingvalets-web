How It Works Using Metro Valet is quick, simple, and seamless. Here’s how it works: 1. Book Your Spot Reserve a valet slot through our app before arriving at the metro station — or just walk in! 2. Drop Off Your Car Drive to the designated Metro Valet zone. Our trained staff will greet you, log your vehicle, and securely park it in our monitored facility. 3. Enjoy Your Day Head straight to your train or destination — no need to waste time circling for parking. 4. Pick Up When You're Back Use the app to notify us when you’re returning. We’ll have your car ready and waiting.
